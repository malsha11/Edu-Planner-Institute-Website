About Edu Planner

Welcome To Edu Planner,

Edu Planner is the number one course provider in Sri Lanka for the BIT External Degree programme awarded by the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC) and has consistently produced some of the best BIT graduates in the country.

Our Vision

To emerge as the most sought-after education and training partner in the region by making a meaningful contribution towards building a knowledgeable society.

Our Mission

Leverage our affiliations and partnerships to empower the youth to shape their lives and create futures for themselves by providing recognised, affordable and up-to-date knowledge.
