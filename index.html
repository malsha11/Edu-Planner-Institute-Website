Welcome to EDU PLANNER INSTITUTE

As the largest, private sector, higher education network in Sri Lanka with 40 centres island-wide, Edu Planner creates opportunities for over 30,000 students every year. Since our inception in 2000, we have built a solid reputation for delivering high quality and affordable academic programmes ranging from certificate to postgraduate level, across a variety of disciplines including Computing, Business, Hospitality, Engineering, Language Training and Personal and Professional Development. Edu Planner is also looking to expand internationally and recently established our first overseas branch in Bangladesh.Hands-on, technology-based education that equips graduates with the skills & knowledge that employers want
